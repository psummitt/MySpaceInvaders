About Me

I'm retired and disabled.

During my 71 years I have been a bus boy, a sailor, a fast food night manager, a computer lab assistant, a bus driver, a cook, a television news photographer, a song writer, a theater manager, a radio DJ, a Marine, a television news videographer, a television field producer, a television engineer, a documentary producer, a college broadcasting instructor, an author, a university broadcasting associate professor, a television news producer, a computer programmer, a web designer, a computer technician, an IT Director, a photographer, and a consultant. I haven't decided what I want to do when I grow up but I know there will be a dog with me.
